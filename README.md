## 🧠 Flagship Projects

### **DuelMinds (private → public soon)**

https://duelminds.com

AI vs AI adversarial debate engine where ideas are tested through structured reasoning.

* Users submit any idea (business, technical, educational, creative, etc.)
* Optional DMS stake to amplify potential rewards
* Two AI agents debate FOR vs AGAINST the idea
* A third AI agent evaluates logic and determines the final verdict
* Outcomes are scored, ranked, and rewarded based on reasoning strength

Explores adversarial AI reasoning, structured evaluation, and incentive-driven idea testing on Solana. Found extremely useful by teachers and students for structured reasoning and argument analysis.

### **Metagolf (acquired — actively in development)**

https://metagolf.world

Physics-driven Unreal Engine game blending real-world golf mechanics with open-water exploration and integrated vehicle traversal.

* Designed in Blender and built in Unreal Engine over three years
* Two large islands connected by an underwater aquarium featuring dynamic marine ecosystems, including sharks, dolphins, and turtles
* Jet skis, boats, and seaplanes integrated directly into gameplay
* Consistent physics powering golf mechanics and all traversal across land, water, and air
* Clubhouse, bar, and competitive leaderboard with real-world reward payouts

A fully realized golfing world built for exploration, precision, and competitive play. Planned for release on Steam soon.

### **SGA (acquired — actively in development)**

https://solanagames.app

A Telegram-native gaming platform combining HTML and Godot games that is designed to onboard one billion monthly users into a Solana-based play-to-earn ecosystem.

* Launching with five titles deployed via Vercel and distributed through Telegram
* Expanding to 200+ games within five years
* Early titles include Machinas and DogRunner (in final pre-production stages)
* Telegram bot integration for instant access, gameplay, and rewards
* SGA-powered progression and incentives across all games

Designed to scale a large Solana-based gaming economy through Telegram, with a growing catalog of games delivered via a bot deployable across group chats for instant gameplay and SGA rewards. Ecosystem scaling model and long-term roadmap: https://solanagames.app/sgapath.html
